About Me

I'm a passionate 3D artist and skilled programmer. I love merging technology with art to build captivating experiences. Whether it's through immersive 3D worlds or seamless code, my goal is always to push creative boundaries. I specialize in Blender, Substance Painter, and Unreal Engine for 3D projects, and JavaScript, Python, and C# for programming. Always learning, always growing.
